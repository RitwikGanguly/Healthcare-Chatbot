Designed and Developed by "The Skill Prodigies" @ Innomatics Research Labs


HealthChat is a Healthcare Chatbot platform and home for RISA (The name of our Chatbot), Built using RASA Conversational AI.
The Chatbot is trained on a clean Dataset with several Diseases and their respective symptoms.
This Chatbot is capable of reverting back with precautions and Medicines for a particular Disease.
NOTE : This Chatbot is purely experimental, Not everything answered by the bot is final or concise and seek medical care. Do not rely completely on this Chatbot.
This Chatbot is for Educational purpose only, not for commerical use.
